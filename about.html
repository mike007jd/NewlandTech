About Newland Communication Tech

Explore our expertise and innovation in IoT and big data solutions.

Who We Are

Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Auckland, New Zealand, Newland Communication Tech is a leading provider of 2G-5G core network solutions, IMS systems, SMS gateways, and wireless communication devices. With offices in Hong Kong and the Philippines, Newland serves a global clientele, leveraging a core team with over 20 years of telecom expertise.

What We Do

1. 2G-5G Core Network Solutions: Scalable and customizable core networks tailored for MNOs, MVNOs, and private enterprise networks.
2. IMS Systems: Powering VoLTE, VoNR, and VOIP services with seamless integration for voice, video, and messaging.
3. SMS Gateways: Enabling SMS functionality across 2G to 5G networks, optimized for real-time delivery and high reliability.

Global Success

Newland has delivered cutting-edge telecom solutions to over 100 clients worldwide, including successful deployments in remote regions such as Palau, Tuvalu, and Norfolk Island. These projects include complete core network setups and 5G network rollouts.

Innovative Applications

1. Mining Networks: Private 5G solutions for underground and surface operations, supporting remote machinery and real-time communication.
2. Indoor 5G Coverage: High-speed, low-latency wireless connectivity using compact femtocell deployments.
3. Industry 4.0: Smart factory solutions with 5G-enabled automation and live equipment monitoring.
